FAQ

Where is my data stored?

All your entries are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in SQLite. Nothing is sent to our servers because we don't have any.

Can I back up or transfer my entries to another device?

iCloud sync is not available in v1.0. Your data is restored only if you back up the device itself via iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder. We may add an explicit export feature in a future version.

What does the Supporter purchase unlock?

In v1.0, the Supporter purchase grants a small badge in your profile and helps fund development. It does not unlock features in v1.0. If future versions add Supporter-only features, existing supporters will receive them automatically.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the App, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I request a refund?

Apple handles all refunds for App Store purchases. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I delete all my data?

Uninstall the App from your device. iOS will delete all locally stored data automatically.
